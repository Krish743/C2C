Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML)
L.D. College of Engineering, Ahmedabad

Overview:
The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) was established in 2020 with the vision of developing engineers capable of building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. The department focuses on core areas such as data science, deep learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning.

Vision:
To become a center of excellence in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning education, research, and innovation.

Mission:
1. To impart strong theoretical and practical knowledge of AI and ML.
2. To foster innovation and research culture among students.
3. To collaborate with industry and research organizations.
4. To develop ethical engineers for societal benefit.

Programs Offered:
- B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (4 years)
- Minor in Artificial Intelligence for other branches

Intake Capacity:
B.Tech (AIML) – 60 students per year

Laboratories:
1. Machine Learning Lab – Equipped with high-performance GPU systems for training deep learning models.
2. Data Science Lab – Contains software like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and JupyterLab.
3. Computer Vision Lab – Used for projects related to image processing and robotics.

Faculty:
The department has 10 faculty members, including 3 Ph.D. holders specializing in AI and Data Analytics. Faculty members are actively involved in publishing research papers and guiding student projects in collaboration with industry partners.

Research Areas:
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Computer Vision
- Reinforcement Learning
- Explainable AI
- AI for Healthcare

Student Activities:
The department organizes regular hackathons, workshops, and seminars on trending technologies. The “AI Club” encourages students to participate in competitions like Smart India Hackathon, Kaggle challenges, and IEEE events.

Placements:
Students of AIML have been placed in top companies like TCS, Infosys, ZS Associates, and Deloitte. Average placement package is around 6 LPA, with the highest package being 18 LPA.
